Return Policy

1. Booking Modifications:

We understand that plans can change. If you need to modify your booking, such as changing dates, accommodations, or adding extras, we will assist you to the best of our ability. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible to discuss your options. Modifications are subject to availability and any applicable fees or rate differences.

2. Booking Cancellations:

Should you need to cancel your booking, please notify us at your earliest convenience. Cancellation policies vary depending on the specific booking, including the type of accommodation, transportation, or activity reserved. We encourage you to review the cancellation policy provided at the time of booking. In some cases, cancellations made within a certain timeframe may be subject to penalties or fees. These fees will be communicated to you before confirming the cancellation.

3. Refunds:

Refunds are issued based on the cancellation policy associated with your booking. If you are eligible for a refund, it will be processed according to the original payment method used at the time of booking. Please note that certain bookings may be non-refundable or subject to partial refunds. Refund eligibility is determined by the terms and conditions set forth by our partners, including hotels, airlines, tour operators, and other service providers. Refunds for eligible cancellations will be processed promptly, but the timing may vary depending on factors such as the payment method and financial institution processing times.

4. Special Circumstances:

We understand that unforeseen circumstances, such as natural disasters, medical emergencies, or other extraordinary events, may impact your travel plans. In such cases, please contact our customer support team for assistance. We will make every effort to accommodate your needs and provide guidance on the available options.

5. Customer Support:

Our dedicated customer support team is available to assist you with any questions, concerns, or requests related to your booking. You can reach us via email, phone, or live chat during our operating hours.

6. Policy Updates:

We reserve the right to update or modify our return policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review our policies periodically for any updates.
